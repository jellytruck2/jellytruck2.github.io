Jelly Truck

Welcome to Jelly Truck, the browser-based physics driving game where wobbly jelly vehicles tackle challenging terrains! Dive into this unique driving experience—no downloads, just pure WebGL-powered fun. Play now and feel the jelly bounce!

Full Screen

Tip: Use the arrow keys to drive your jelly truck. Click “Play Fullscreen” for the best experience.

Drive your jelly truck across ramps and uneven terrain while managing speed and balance to avoid flipping over. The soft jelly body reacts realistically to collisions and changes in momentum, creating an engaging and fun driving challenge.

How to Play

  • Arrow Keys: Use left and right arrows to steer your jelly truck
  • Up Arrow: Accelerate forward
  • Down Arrow: Brake or reverse
  • Spacebar: Use special boost (if available in certain levels)
  • Try to complete each level as fast as possible without tipping over

About Jelly Truck

Jelly Truck is a one-of-a-kind driving game featuring deformable jelly-like vehicles that stretch, bounce, and wobble through exciting levels. Built with cutting-edge WebGL technology, it runs directly in your browser without any downloads or installations.

Key Features

  • Realistic jelly physics with smooth vehicle deformation
  • Multiple challenging levels filled with ramps, bumps, and obstacles
  • Optimized for fast performance on desktop and mobile browsers
  • No downloads or installations—play instantly online
  • Fun and intuitive controls for players of all ages
